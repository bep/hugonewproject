---
title: "Notes from a slow Sunday"
date: 2025-10-08
---

Rain since nine in the morning. The kind that does not commit to itself. I made coffee twice, read half a chapter of a book I am not sure I like, and watched a neighbour reorganise their garage through the kitchen window for what must have been an hour. He did not seem to be making progress.

I think about productivity less on days like this, and the result is that more gets done. Not by me, of course. The neighbour finished the garage by lunch.
