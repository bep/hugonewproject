---
title: "Why the cheap notebook beats the expensive one"
date: 2025-12-02
---

I bought a beautiful notebook in October. Cloth cover, sewn binding, paper with a faint cream tint that made every pen look better than it was. I used it for two weeks and then I stopped, because I could not bring myself to write anything ordinary in it.

The cheap one I have been using since is the kind they sell in packs of five at the supermarket. I do not feel anything when I write in it. That, it turns out, is exactly what I want from a notebook. The ones I fill up the fastest are the ones I do not feel guilty about wasting.

The beautiful notebook is still on the shelf, half empty, waiting for thoughts that deserve it.
